About Me

Aisha Fadalalla is an IT student at AkiraChix, Nairobi-Kenya, majoring in software development and UI/UX Design. Being born and raised in Sudan, it was not easy for her to decide what career path she wanted to take. This is because ladies were expected to be housewives and nothing more than that. This motivated her to step up and do something different for herself which she believes would save the coming generations. Her hunger for knowledge and determination led her to technology.

Currently, she is working on several projects both group and personal projects in design, web, and mobile development. This has helped her build her confidence, grow her collaboration skills and leadership skills.

Her ambitions and goals in the future are to become a great software developer and UX/UX designer to bring change to her community and also contribute to Africa’s development. She also wants to become a good role model to inspire other young ladies to join the tech industry. For more information click Resume.
